Who I Am

I am a 26 year old web developer interested in human cognition and how we interact with technology. I spend most of my time working at a startup with some respected colleagues and close friends to build awesome web applications for our clients.
When I'm not building web sites I like to make stuff with my hands, work on cars, and explore places I've never been.

What I Do

With over six years consulting experience architecting websites and web applications of all shapes and sizes I have learned to manage budgets, clients, people, and most importantly expectations with ease. My passion is in designing user interfaces that people can naturally interact with, and creating simple elegant solutions to complex problems. Here are some of the things I do in a typical day at the office:

  • Design wireframes
  • Identify and document requirements
  • Manage schedules & resources
  • Research & implement CMS systems
  • Write proposals
  • Learn new things (Currently: Android SDK & Ruby on Rails)
